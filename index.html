What This Is
Built for the Igala People
The first English-Igala translator built with verified translations from native speakers across Ankpa, Dekina, and Kogi East Agbale — with proper tone marks and regional variations documented.
How It Works
People First, Technology Second
Every word in this translator was contributed and verified by fluent Igala speakers. The technology connects those words into natural sentences that reflect how Igala is actually spoken.
The Vision
Language Lives On
From schools in Kogi State to Igala communities in the diaspora, this tool exists to keep the Igala language alive, accessible, and growing in the digital age.
